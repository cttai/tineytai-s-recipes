    • Matcha Glutinous Rice Cake

    INGREDIENTS

    • 16 ounce package glutinous rice flour
    • 2 Tablespoons matcha powder
    • 2 cups milk
    • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
    • 1/2 cup sugar, more or less (to taste)
    • 3 eggs, beaten

      • Another recipe to try (Plain)
    • 1 bag glutinous rice flour (400 g)
    • 1 Tablespoon baking powder
    • 2 1/2 cups milk or coconut milk
    • 3 eggs
    • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
    • 1 cup sugar

    DIRECTIONS

    1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
    2. Blend the matcha and milk together to make sure the matcha dissolves.
    3. Beat eggs, add the matcha milk, and combine the rest of the ingredients.
    4. Pour batter into a gresed pan.
    5. Bake for 50-60 minutes

    Enjoy!
