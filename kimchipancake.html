Kimchi Pancake

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 ounces chopped kimchi
  • 1/3 small onion diced
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup water
  • green onion
  • chili flakes / gochugaru (optional)

DIRECTIONS

  1. Chop onion and kimchi into small pieces.
  2. Mix kimchi, onion, green onion, water, flour in a bowl.
  3. In a large saucepan, add a generous amount of oil over medium-high.
  4. Add batter and distribute evenly over the whole pan.
  5. Cook each side for 3 minutes and serve. Sprinkle some chili flakes for extra heat if desired.

Enjoy!
