Spinach Bread

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups old-fashioned oats
  • ¼ cup ground flaxseed
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ½ cup sugar
  • ½ cup almond milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups packed spinach leaves (~225g?)
  • ¼ cup oil
  • 2 large bananas
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit and grease the pan you will be using.
  2. Put 2 cups of old-fashioned oats in your blender (Vitamix) and blend until the oats achieve the texture of flour. Transfer to a medium mixing bowl.
  3. Add the rest of the dry ingredients to the oat flour (ground flaxseed, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon and sugar). Set aside.
  4. Add almond milk to the blender followed by 2 cups of spinach. Blend together until completely smooth and homogenous.
  5. Add bananas and coconut oil and blend until smooth.
  6. Add vanilla and eggs and blend until just combined
  7. Pour wet ingredients to dry mixture and stir to combine (making sure there are no lumps)!
  8. Pour mixture into pan. Bake for 35-40 minutes if using a bundt pan or until toothpick comes out clean and the top springs back when touched. If using a loaf pan, bake for 50 to 60 minutes. If using a muffin tin, bake for 20-25 minutes.

Enjoy!
