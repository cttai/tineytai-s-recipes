Eggs

Oils/Fats

Applesauce, Avocados, Bananas, Greek Yogurt make great subsitutes for oils, butter, etc

Veggies

You can almost always subsitute similar vegetables if you don't have the specific one mentioned in the recipe.

Fun Fact #1

Onions that are chilled before chopping give off less of the chemical reaction that makes you tear up.

Fun Fact #2

You can cover apple or avocado slices in lemon juice and store them in an airtight container. This will keep the fruit from browning for about a day.

Tip #1

Confused when to use baking soda versus baking powder? Remember this:

Soda = Spread -- (ex: cookies)

Powder = Puff | (ex: cakes)

Tip #2

Play with mix-ins! Feel free to add nuts, dried fruit, chocolate chips, etc when baking! You may need to adjust the amount of flour or liquid to make sure it's not too dry.
