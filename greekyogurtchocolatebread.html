Greek Yogurt Chocolate Bread

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • ½ cup dutch processed cocoa
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 egg
  • ¼ cup oil
  • ¼ cup Greek Yogurt
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • ½ cup milk
  • 1 cup coffee
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Totally optional: ½ cup nuts like chopped walnuts

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit and grease the pan you will be using.
  2. In a bowl whisk together the eggs, greek yogurt, milk, coffee, oil, and vanilla until well mixed.
  3. Sift the cocoa if it has lumps, flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda and salt. Fold with a spatula until just combined.
  4. Fold in chocolate chips and optional mixins like walnuts
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared pan.
  6. Bake for 35 minutes if using a bundt pan. If using a loaf pan, bake for 50 to 60 minutes.

Enjoy!
