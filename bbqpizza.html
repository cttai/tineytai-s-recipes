BBQ Pizza

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup Chicken breast or thigh
  • 1/2 small red onion sliced
  • 1 cup Spinach
  • 1/3 cup + 2 Tablespoon BBQ sauce
  • Pizza Dough
  • 1 cup Shredded Cheese
  • Chili flakes / gochugaru (optional)
  • Cilantro (optional)

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees Farenheit with a flat baking pan inside.
  2. Chop onion into small slices. Chop chicken into small pieces.
  3. Stir fry chicken until fully cooked. Add 2 Tablespoons of BBQ sauce and mix until well combined.
  4. Add in spinach and remove from heat when spinach wilts
  5. Stretch pizza dough until it is the desired shape. Spread a generous amount of BBQ sauce until surface is fully covered and sprinkle a thin layer of shredded cheese. Add the chicken and spinach mixture over the surface. Add the sliced onion and the desired amount of cheese.
  6. Transfer pizza to the hot baking pan inside oven and cook for about 12-15 minutes or until there is browning. Remove from pan and sprinkle some chili flakes for extra heat and/or cilantro if desired.

Enjoy!
